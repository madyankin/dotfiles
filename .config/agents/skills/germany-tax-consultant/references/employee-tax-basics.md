# Employee Tax Basics In Germany

Use this reference for common employee-facing tax return questions.

## Scope

- private individuals
- employees
- common annual income tax return questions

## Core Concepts

- annual income tax return: `Einkommensteuererklaerung`
- income-related expenses: `Werbungskosten`
- special expenses: `Sonderausgaben`
- extraordinary burdens: `aussergewoehnliche Belastungen`
- wage tax certificate: `Lohnsteuerbescheinigung`
- tax assessment notice: `Steuerbescheid` or `Einkommensteuerbescheid`

## Typical Documents

- `Lohnsteuerbescheinigung`
- health and pension contribution certificates
- receipts for work-related expenses
- donation receipts
- childcare cost records when relevant
- commuting and home office records when relevant

## Common User Goals

- understand whether filing is required or optional
- know what documents to collect
- understand what employee expenses can often be deducted
- get help entering data into ELSTER
- understand the difference between withholding and the final annual tax

## Guidance

- Treat `Steuerklasse` as payroll withholding context, not the final annual tax result by itself.
- Distinguish clearly between common situations and exceptions.
- If the user has only employment income and straightforward deductions, provide structured practical guidance.
- If the user also has business income, freelance income, complex foreign income, or major investment issues, escalate out of scope.

## Bilingual Style

- Explain in English first.
- Add the main German term on first mention.
- Use the German term directly if the user will see it in ELSTER or on official documents.
