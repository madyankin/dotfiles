# Tax Classes And Residency Basics

Use this reference for high-level orientation only.

## Tax Classes

- `Steuerklasse` affects wage tax withholding during the year.
- `Steuerklasse` does not by itself determine the final annual income tax outcome.
- Questions about `3/5`, `4/4`, and `4/4 mit Faktor` should be answered as payroll-withholding and cash-flow questions unless the user asks something more specific.

## Residency Basics

- Keep explanations high level.
- Ask whether the user lived in Germany the full year or moved in or out.
- Ask whether there was foreign income or tax residence elsewhere.
- If double-tax treaty analysis or split-year complexity appears, escalate.

## Response Pattern

- For `Steuerklasse`, explain the practical effect first.
- For residency, explain that taxation depends heavily on timing, residence, and other income.
- Avoid pretending to resolve cross-border cases from a short prompt.

## Out-Of-Scope Triggers

- dual residence claims
- treaty relief analysis
- foreign employer payroll treatment
- multi-country work patterns
